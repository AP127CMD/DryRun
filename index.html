FPL — ACTIVE FLIGHT PLAN

DIRECT-TO: [D→] → small knob spins char, big knob moves cursor → ENT → ENT (activate).

LOAD FPL: FPL → cursor ON (push FMS knob) → spell waypoint → ENT … MENU → Store Flight Plan.

CDI SOURCE: softkey CDI toggles GPS (magenta) / VLOC (green). For this VFR XC stay GPS.

NRST: NRST page → nearest airports with BRG/DIS/rwy — your engine-failure friend. Drill: from any point of the route, find the nearest field in under 10 seconds.

OBS: suspends auto-sequencing — use to hold over a fix. Push OBS again to resume.

Fuel: FADEC/engine page — cross-check fuel flow vs the PERF tab plan at every FREDA.
